Welcome

WELCOME TO THE RAILROAD HISTORICAL MUSEUM INC. Springfield Missouri. We are a nonprofit museum dedicated to the preservation of Railroad History and the heritage of the past, educating present and future generations.

Come Join Us!

With NO charge for admission and our air conditioned cars, you are always welcome to view history! In addition to general admision, we offer both private tours and parties by reservation. To request, please submit a reservation request to reserve the train today.
