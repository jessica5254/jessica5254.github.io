Tours

Private tours are free and available weekdays by appointment only, for educational, charitable, civic groups and families. You are welcome to explore the Frisco Locomotive 4524, Commuter Car, Caboose, and the depot.

We have artifacts, equipment, photos, paintings and much more from the St. Louis San-Francisco Railroad and other Railroads. There are many hands-on activities for visitors of all ages. We also have videos of Steam and Diesel Trains, and a Model Train Layout.

The staff at the museum are volunteers, many of whom have a connection to the railroad industry and can answer your questions. Our passionate volunteers and resident experts would be happy to make your visit an enlightening one!
